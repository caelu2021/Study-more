  • Enter homework, projects, tests and assignments as soon as they are assigned
  • Practice active listening by concentrating on what’s being said and taking notes in your own words.
  • Be aware of what distracts you in class and know how to steer clear of these distractions.
  • Talk to friends or the teacher if you have missed a class to ensure your notes are complete.
  • If you don’t feel comfortable asking in front of everyone, write yourself a reminder to talk to the teacher after class.

When making a study schedule, look at your planner and think about what needs to be accomplished. Think about the types of questions that will be on the test and the topics that will be covered so you know what you should focus on.
